## Training Data Requirements by Domain

### Minimum Dataset Sizes
- **Text classification**: 1,000-10,000 examples
- **Language modeling**: 1M-1B tokens
- **Image classification**: 10,000-1M images
- **Object detection**: 5,000-100,000 annotated images
- **Machine translation**: 1M-100M sentence pairs
- **Question answering**: 10,000-100,000 QA pairs

### Data Quality Standards
1. **Accuracy**: >95% label correctness
2. **Diversity**: Comprehensive coverage of edge cases
3. **Balance**: Avoid severe class imbalance
4. **Cleanliness**: Remove duplicates and noise
5. **Representativeness**: Match real-world distribution
6. **Recency**: Keep data up-to-date
