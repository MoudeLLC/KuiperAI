## 🛡️ Safety Features

### Content Categories
- **SAFE** - General safe content

- **HARMFUL** - Blocked

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Dataset not found"
Run: `python3 learn_from_world.py` first

### "Model not found"
Run: `python3 train_comprehensive.py` first

### "Tests failing"
Check: `python3 comprehensive_test.py` for details

### "Out of memory"
Reduce model size in training script:
- `d_model=128` (instead of 256)
- `num_layers=2` (instead of 4)
- `batch_size=4` (instead of 8)
